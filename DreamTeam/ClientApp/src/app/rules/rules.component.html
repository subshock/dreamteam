Rules

Follow the rules and nobody gets hurt.

  • A team must have 12 players
  • Teams must include a nominated Captain and Vice Captain
  • Captains receive double points
  • If the Captain does not play, then the Vice Captain will receive double points
  • The top 11 players from each round earn points for the team
  • The total team value must not exceed the salary cap
  • All entries must be paid up before commencement of the season
  • Incomplete / Rain affected matches will be included
  • All female players earn double points
  • A maximum of 3 player substitutions can be made during trade periods
  • Substitutions must be finalised before the end of the trade period.
  • No other player substitutions can be made
  • Substitutions must abide by the salary cap
  • BDCA Finals and Twenty20 competitions are not included
  • Captain / Vice Captain may only be changed during a trade period
  • The Giraffe has the final say
